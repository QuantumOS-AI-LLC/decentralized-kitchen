No commercial kitchen is required for Option A! Local stay-at-home cooks are registered under the Arizona Cottage Food Program (Title 36), which allows preparation of non-hazardous food products in home kitchens. GKIA helps audit and register your cooks to ensure compliance.

The GKIA CRM dashboard integrates with delivery APIs. Orders from Uber Eats, Grubhub, and our Web App are compiled in the CRM, which auto-assigns local gig drivers to pick up from the cook's home and drop off at the consumer or roach coach.

It is preconfigured with advanced system prompts, geofenced Phoenix casino traffic logs, Spanish-language marketing campaign templates, and database hook scripts. Claude analyzes live CRM metrics to suggest route optimizations, quality checks, and ad spends.
